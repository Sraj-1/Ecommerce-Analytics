# Business Questions

The following questions define the scope of analysis for this consulting engagement.

---

## Sales Analysis

* Are sales increasing over time?
* Which months generate the highest revenue?
* Which product categories contribute the most revenue?
* Which cities and states generate the highest sales?
* What is the average order value?

---

## Customer Analysis

* Who are the highest-value customers?
* What percentage of customers make repeat purchases?
* Which customers are at risk of churn?
* What is the average customer lifetime value?
* Which locations have the most active customers?

---

## Product Analysis

* Which products generate the highest revenue?
* Which categories are underperforming?
* Which products receive poor customer reviews?
* Which products sell frequently but generate low revenue?

---

## Seller Analysis

* Which sellers contribute the most revenue?
* Which sellers consistently receive low ratings?
* Which sellers experience the highest delivery delays?
* How concentrated is revenue among sellers?

---

## Logistics Analysis

* What is the average delivery time?
* Which regions experience frequent delivery delays?
* Does delayed delivery reduce customer satisfaction?
* Which delivery stages contribute most to delays?

---

## Payment Analysis

* Which payment methods are most frequently used?
* Do payment methods influence order value?
* Are installment payments associated with higher-value purchases?

---

## Customer Experience Analysis

* What is the average customer review score?
* Which factors influence customer satisfaction?
* How do delivery delays impact reviews?
* Which product categories receive the highest and lowest ratings?
