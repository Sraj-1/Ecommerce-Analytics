# Success Metrics

The success of this project will be measured using the following business and analytical metrics.

---

## Financial Metrics

* Total Revenue
* Gross Profit
* Profit Margin
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Monthly Revenue Growth

---

## Customer Metrics

* Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
* Customer Retention Rate
* Repeat Purchase Rate
* Customer Churn Rate
* Average Customer Rating

---

## Sales Metrics

* Orders per Month
* Revenue by Category
* Revenue by Region
* Top Performing Products
* Top Performing Sellers

---

## Operational Metrics

* Average Delivery Time
* Late Delivery Rate
* Order Fulfillment Time
* On-Time Delivery Percentage

---

## Product Metrics

* Category Performance
* Product Sales Volume
* Product Rating
* Product Revenue Contribution

---

## Seller Metrics

* Seller Revenue
* Seller Rating
* Seller Delivery Performance
* Seller Order Volume

---

## Project Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if it provides actionable recommendations that help stakeholders:

* Identify the key drivers of profitability
* Improve customer retention
* Reduce delivery delays
* Increase operational efficiency
* Support strategic business decisions through data-driven insights
