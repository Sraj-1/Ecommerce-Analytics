# Business Understanding

## Project Title

**E-Commerce Business Intelligence & Profitability Analysis**

---

## Client

**Olist** is a Brazilian e-commerce marketplace that connects customers with thousands of independent sellers across Brazil. The platform enables sellers to reach customers while providing end-to-end order processing, payment, and delivery services.

---

## Business Background

Over the past few years, Olist has experienced steady growth in sales and customer orders. Despite this growth, the company has observed inconsistencies in profitability and customer satisfaction. Operational challenges such as delivery delays, varying seller performance, and changing customer behavior have made it difficult for executives to identify where improvements are needed.

The executive team has engaged our analytics consulting team to investigate the underlying causes and provide data-driven recommendations that support better business decisions.

---

## Business Problem Statement

Although revenue continues to grow, business profitability remains inconsistent. Management lacks clear visibility into the operational and commercial factors affecting profitability.

Key concerns include:

* Increasing delivery delays
* Declining customer satisfaction
* Uneven seller performance
* Limited understanding of customer purchasing behavior
* Difficulty identifying profitable products and categories

---

## Primary Objective

Increase business profitability through data-driven insights and actionable recommendations.

---

## Secondary Objectives

* Improve customer retention
* Reduce delivery delays
* Increase customer satisfaction
* Identify high-value customers
* Evaluate seller performance
* Optimize product category performance
* Improve operational efficiency
* Support executive decision-making

---

## Expected Business Outcome

This project will provide analytical insights that help stakeholders:

* Understand revenue and order trends
* Identify profitability drivers
* Improve logistics performance
* Enhance customer experience
* Optimize product and seller strategies
* Support strategic business decisions through interactive dashboards and reports
