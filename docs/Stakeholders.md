# Stakeholders

The following stakeholders will use the insights generated from this project.

| Stakeholder              | Responsibilities                   | Key Questions                                  | KPIs                                     |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| CEO                      | Overall business strategy          | Is the business growing profitably?            | Revenue, Profit, Growth Rate             |
| CFO                      | Financial performance              | Which areas reduce profitability?              | Profit Margin, Costs                     |
| Sales Manager            | Sales performance                  | Which products and categories perform best?    | Sales Revenue, Average Order Value       |
| Marketing Manager        | Customer acquisition and retention | Which customers should be targeted?            | Customer Retention, Repeat Purchase Rate |
| Operations Manager       | Order fulfillment and logistics    | Where are delivery delays occurring?           | Delivery Time, Late Delivery Rate        |
| Inventory Manager        | Product planning                   | Which products require inventory optimization? | Inventory Turnover, Product Demand       |
| Customer Support Manager | Customer experience                | Why are customers dissatisfied?                | Average Review Score, Complaint Rate     |
| Seller Success Team      | Seller management                  | Which sellers need improvement?                | Seller Rating, Seller Performance        |
